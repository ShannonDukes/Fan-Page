    • Trivia

    Opeth was formed in 1989 in Stockholm, Sweden by lead vocalist David Isberg.

    The band name was derived from the word "Opet", taken from the Wilbur Smith novel "The Sunbird".

    In this novel, Opet is the name of a fictional Phenician city in South Africa translated as "City of the Moon".
